DOMESTIC WASTE:


Waste that is generated as a result of the ordinary day-to-day use of a domestic premise and is either: taken from the premises by or on behalf of the occupier who generated the waste; without consideration.


Factors:


* Lack of public awareness

* Refusal to learn about compliance

* Insufficient Investment in Waste management

* Lack of proper machinery


How to reduce the Domestic Wastes:


* Awareness and Education

* Waste Reduction

* Recycling

* Composting

* Waste Collection and Disposal

* Hazardous Waste Management

* E-Waste Management

* Illegal Dumping Prevention
